# Deliverable 3 --- Screen 1 (Command Center) Notes

*Last Updated: July 2026*

## Philosophy

The Command Center is not a dashboard.

It is the owner's operating console.

Its purpose is to answer one question within five seconds:

> **What relationship deserves my attention next, why, and what is the
> best action to advance it?**

The Command Center never thinks in channels. It thinks in Relationships.

# Relationships Screen

## Purpose

Owns relationship strategy.

Defines:

-   Relationship Goal
-   Relationship Stage
-   Commercial Objective
-   Default Goals
-   Individual Overrides

Bulk default goals apply to all relationships unless explicitly
overridden. Defaults live at the organisation level (default_goal,
default_lifecycle_target); individual relationships may override
either.

Examples of overrides:

-   Receive Metro RFQ
-   Become Preferred Backup Supplier
-   Schedule Technical Meeting

Reaching a goal never removes a relationship from the system — it
advances the relationship to its next lifecycle stage and a new
operating cadence, per Constitution doctrine 13.

Relationships may also be assigned to a specific user
(relationships.owner_user_id) for future multi-user work
distribution, though no assignment UI exists yet.

The Command Center only reads these values.

# Relationship Advisor

Replace simple message generation with an AI advisor.

Purpose:

Help the owner think.

The advisor evaluates:

-   Relationship history
-   Current stage
-   Commercial objective
-   Previous interactions
-   Owner voice
-   Company positioning

The advisor should:

-   Challenge poor ideas
-   Recommend better approaches
-   Explain why
-   Then draft communication

Primary objective:

Improve judgement, not merely generate text.

RIOS optimizes for better relationship decisions.
