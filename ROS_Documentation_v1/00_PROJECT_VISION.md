# ROS --- Project Vision

## Purpose

ROS (Relationship Operating System) is a Relationship Intelligence Operating System.

Its purpose is to continuously evaluate every business relationship, determine which relationships can be meaningfully advanced today, generate context-aware communications in the owner's voice, preserve institutional relationship memory, and guide every relationship toward a defined commercial objective.

ROS exists to help an owner maintain thousands of meaningful business relationships without relying on memory.

The system does not behave like a traditional CRM. Its primary
responsibility is to decide **who deserves attention today, why, and
what the next best action should be**.

## Product Principles

-   Relationship-first, not pipeline-first.
-   Events over static fields.
-   Work Items over reminders.
-   Human approval for meaningful communication.
-   AI assists; deterministic business logic governs.
-   Every feature must be additive.

## Non-goals

-   Not an ERP.
-   Not an accounting system.
-   Not a generic marketing automation platform.
