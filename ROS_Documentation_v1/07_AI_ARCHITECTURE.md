# AI Architecture

AI responsibilities

-   Summarization
-   Draft generation
-   Classification
-   Context synthesis

AI never: - schedules work - changes database rules - bypasses approval

Context sources

-   Voice profile
-   Company profile
-   Timeline
-   Interaction history
-   Knowledge base

## AI Mission

AI does not decide WHAT should happen.

AI decides HOW to communicate once deterministic business logic has selected the Relationship and its current objective.

AI optimizes for:

- Owner Voice
- Company Positioning
- Relationship Stage
- Commercial Objective
- Historical Memory

AI never owns scheduling, prioritization or business rules.
